Steps to Becoming a Motorcyclist

  1. Research and understand the risks
  2. Take the written learners permit test
  3. Practice riding skills
      -Borrow a motorcycle from a trusted friend
      -You have up to 6 months to practice
  4. Take your riding skills test at the DMV
  5. Ride safe
